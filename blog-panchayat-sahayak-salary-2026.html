पंचायत सहायक Salary 2026 — ₹6,000 Manadey, PFMS Payment & Kab Milti Hai

📅 28 March 2026 ⏱️ 5 min read ✍️ gpsahayak.in Team 🏷️ Salary Guide
📌 Quick Summary: UP Panchayat Sahayak ki monthly salary (manadey) ₹6,000 per month hai. Yeh Gram Nidhi (Gram Panchayat fund) ke zariye PFMS ke through bank account mein credit hoti hai. Attendance App par daily hajiri zaroori hai.

1. पंचायत सहायक की Salary (Manadey) कितनी है?

Uttar Pradesh mein Panchayat Sahayak cum Data Entry Operator (DEO) ki monthly salary — jise officially "Manadey" (मानदेय) kaha jata hai — ₹6,000 per month hai (as of 2026).

Yeh koi PF ya ESI wali government job nahi hai — yeh ek honorary/contractual position hai jisme fixed monthly maandey milta hai.

VivranDetails
Monthly Manadey₹6,000
Annual Total₹72,000
Payment SourceGram Nidhi → PFMS
Payment MethodDirect Bank Transfer (DBT)
Attendance RequirementDaily (Attendance App)
StateUttar Pradesh Only

2. Salary Kab Milti Hai?

Panchayat Sahayak ki salary generally har mahine ke 10–20 tarikh ke beech process hoti hai, lekin state government ke instructions aur fund availability ke hisab se yeh change ho sakti hai.

⚠️ Dhyan Den: Salary aane mein delay ke main kaaran hain — Attendance App par hajiri na lagna, Gram Panchayat ke Mukhiya ya Sachiv ka approval pending hona, ya PFMS mein bank account details galat hona.

3. PFMS se Payment Status Kaise Check Karein?

Apni salary payment ka status check karne ke liye PFMS (Public Financial Management System) ka use karein:

1
pfms.nic.in kholeinBrowser mein type karein: pfms.nic.in — official PFMS website khulegi.
2
"Know Your Payment" click kareinHomepage par "Know Your Payment" ka option milega — use click karein.
3
Bank Details bhareinApna Bank Name, Account Number aur Word Verification (Captcha) bharein.
4
Submit karein"Submit" button dabane par aapki latest payment history aur amount dikh jayegi.
✅ Tip: Agar PFMS par payment nahi dikh rahi, to apne Gram Panchayat ke Sachiv se PFMS beneficiary ID verify karwayein. Galat bank account number bhi ek common issue hai.

4. Salary Band Kyon Hoti Hai? Kaise Kholein?

Kai baar Panchayat Sahayak ki salary "band" ho jati hai — matlab payment ruk jati hai. Iske kaaran aur solutions:

KaaranSolution
Attendance App par hajiri nahi lagayiDaily morning & evening app update karein
Mukhiya/Sachiv ka approval pendingOffice jakar approval karwayein
Bank account details galatPFMS mein sahi account update karwayein
e-Gram Swaraj data incompleteSabhi mandatory entries complete karein
Gram Nidhi mein fund nahiBlock office se contact karein

5. Salary Badhegi? Kya Upcoming Update Hai?

Kai Panchayat Sahayak associations ne salary badhakar ₹10,000–₹15,000 karne ki maang ki hai. Abhi tak koi official order nahi aaya, lekin 2026 mein UP government ki review committee is mudde par vichar kar rahi hai.

Latest orders ke liye hamesha is portal ke Notices section ko check karte rahein.

❓ Aksar Puche Jaane Wale Sawaal (FAQ)

पंचायत सहायक की salary kitni hai 2026 mein?
2026 mein UP Panchayat Sahayak ki monthly salary (manadey) ₹6,000 per month hai — yani ₹72,000 per year. Yeh directly bank account mein PFMS ke zariye bheja jata hai.
Salary band ho gayi hai — kya karein?
Pehle Attendance App check karein (daily hajiri complete hai?). Phir Gram Sachiv se milein aur PFMS mein apna account status check karein. Block PRD office mein complaint bhi de sakte hain.
PFMS par payment nahi dikh rahi — kyon?
Ya to payment abhi process nahi hui, ya PFMS mein account number galat hai. Sahiv se apni beneficiary ID verify karwayein.
Kya Panchayat Sahayak ko PF ya pension milti hai?
Abhi nahi. Panchayat Sahayak ek contractual/honorary position hai — PF, ESI ya pension nahi milti. Iske liye state sarkar se maang chal rahi hai.
Salary kab tak badhegi Panchayat Sahayak ki?
Abhi tak koi official GO nahi aaya hai. UP ke kuch janpratinidhiyon ne ₹10,000 karne ki maang rakhi hai. Latest update ke liye is portal ke Notices section check karte rahein.
